### **گرامر زبان انگلیسی**  
1. Which of the following sentences correctly uses the subjunctive mood?  
   A) If I were you, I would accept the offer.  
   B) If I was you, I would accept the offer.  
   C) If she was here, we would have won.  
   D) If he would study, he could pass.  

2. Which sentence correctly applies the rule of parallelism?  
   A) She enjoys reading, to swim, and jogging.  
   B) She enjoys reading, swimming, and jogging.  
   C) She enjoys to read, swimming, and jogging.  
   D) She enjoys reading, to swim, and to jog.  

3. Identify the sentence with the correct usage of a restrictive clause.  
   A) The book, which I borrowed from the library, is interesting.  
   B) The book that I borrowed from the library is interesting.  
   C) The book, that I borrowed from the library, is interesting.  
   D) The book which I borrowed from the library is interesting.  

4. Choose the sentence that correctly uses a past perfect tense.  
   A) By the time he arrived, I had finished my work.  
   B) By the time he arrives, I had finished my work.  
   C) By the time he arrived, I finish my work.  
   D) By the time he arrived, I have finished my work.  

5. Which sentence correctly follows subject-verb agreement rules?  
   A) Either John or his friends are coming to the party.  
   B) Either John or his friends is coming to the party.  
   C) Neither John nor his friends was coming to the party.  
   D) Neither John nor his friends is coming to the party.
